## üé¨ VIDEO UPLOAD - EASY STEPS

**Current Status:** Video slot ready, waiting for your video file

### How to Add Your Video (Super Simple!)

1. **Upload your video to Google Drive:**
   - Go to https://drive.google.com
   - Upload your property tour video
   - Right-click ‚Üí Get link
   - Make sure it's set to "Anyone with the link can view"

2. **Get the video URL:**
   - Your link looks like: `https://drive.google.com/file/d/ABC123XYZ/view`
   - Convert to: `https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=ABC123XYZ`
   - (Copy the part after `/d/` and before `/view` - that's your ID)

3. **Paste in your website:**
   - Open `/data/content.ts`
   - Find line with `videoUrl: "PASTE_YOUR_VIDEO_URL_HERE"`
   - Replace with your URL
   - Save!

**That's it! Your video will appear in the gallery!**
